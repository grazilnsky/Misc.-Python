Angular Resolution Help

What?

Angular Resolution is an application which displays 2 parallel lines and with the help of 2 sliders. These sliders allow you to change the distance between the lines, or the thickness of the lines. The angular view from your perspective can be computed by pressing the compute button.

How?

Use sliders(fig.1, fig.2) to change the distance between lines(fig.1) and the thickness(fig.2) of the lines. When you will see 1 line, the box(fig.3) write the distance(in centimeters) from the eye to the screen and press “Calculate” button(fig.4) in order to compute your angular view.
