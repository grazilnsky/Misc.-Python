Memory Test Help

What?

This application is a memory game where the user has to remember 5 words or numbers that appear on a canvas and disappear after a short time.

How?

Click either one of the radio boxes, then press the "Start" button. Try to memorize the words that appear on the canvas, and follow the instructions written there. Write your answer and click the "Confirm Answer" button. Note that you cannot change between numbers and words until you finish the game for your first option. You can freely switch between them after each game completion.

Example of an answer

Why?

Memory tests are a good way to test your short term memory, as well as the speed at which your brain can process words or numbers.
